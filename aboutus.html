About Us

Vorskins is a visionary beauty-retail concept founded in Indonesia by Childe Mawlester in 2021. Vorskins unique, open-sell environment features an ever-increasing amount of classic and emerging brands across a broad range of product categories including skincare, makeup, fragrance, and body. in addition to vorskins' own private label. Today, Vorskins is not only the leading chain of perfume and cosmetics stores in Indonesia but also a powerful beauty presence in some neighboring countries. Vorskins is highly regarded as a beauty trailblazer thanks to its unparalleled assortment of prestige products, unbiased service from experts, interactive shopping environment, disruptive spirit and constant innovation. Vorskins operates approximately 1,900 stores in 29 countries worldwide, with an expanding base of over 200 stores across the Asia Pacific region including Australia, China, Singapore, Malaysia, Thailand, Indonesia & India.

Vision

to create a beliefs in which beauty has many different forms and every single person has the freedom to set their own definition of beauty based on personal preference without any societal expectation or bias to reveal not only the beauty outside but also the beauty inside.

Mission

Our goal is to offer each and every person around the world the best of beauty in terms of quality, efficacy, safety, sincerity and responsibility to satisfy all beauty needs and desires in their infinite diversity. we harness the power of our innovation to continually enhance the performance of our products and services. we leverage each of our brands to celebrate all expressions of beauty. we anchor our actions in our strong values and demanding ethical principles.
